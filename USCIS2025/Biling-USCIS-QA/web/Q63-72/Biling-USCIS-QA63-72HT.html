Question 63

  • You don't have to pay (a poll tax) to vote.
  • Any citizen can vote. (Women and men can vote.)
  • A male citizen of any race (can vote).

  • Ou pa oblije peye (yon taks elektoral) pou w vote.
  • Nenpòt sitwayen kapab vote. (Fanm kou gason kapab vote).
  • Yon sitwayen gason kèlkeswa ras li kapab vote.
