Bilingual Study Aid for 2025 Citizenship Questions

Each of the 128 questions and answers from the 2025 Citizenship test is presented with the English text and audio clip first. This is because the English test is required of most people who apply for US Citizenship. Audio was synthesized from the English text. In this bilingual resource, the Spanish text has been translated from the English unofficially, because USCIS had not provided a Spanish-language resource. Audio was synthesized from the Spanish text.

This set of resources is not official USCIS content. Everyone who uses this set of study materials should refer first to the official website for the Citizenship test study materials on this webpage Study for the Test. There are official PDFs of the English test questions and the Study Guide that you can download from the USCIS page.

To understand the relationship between the questions and the Study Guide, please use the cross-reference provided with these materials, Self-study Resources for the New Citizenship Test.

Kreyòl Ayisyen

Èd Etid Bileng pou Kesyon Sitwayènte 2025 yo

Chak nan 128 kesyon ak repons ki soti nan egzamen Sitwayènte 2025 la prezante an premye avèk tèks ak odyo an anglè. Sa se paske egzamen an anglè a obligatwa pou pifò moun ki aplike pou Sitwayènte Etazini. Odyo a te sentèz apati tèks angle a. Nan resous bileng sa a, tèks kreyòl la te tradwi apati angle a yon fason ki pa ofisyèl, paske USCIS pa t bay yon resous an kreyòl. Odyo a te sentèz apati tèks kreyòl la.

Ansanm resous sa yo pa se kontni ofisyèl USCIS. Tout moun ki itilize ansanm materyèl etid sa yo ta dwe konsilte premye sit entènèt ofisyèl pou materyèl etid egzamen Sitwayènte a nan paj entènèt sa a Study for the Test. Gen PDF ofisyèl pou kesyon egzamen an anglè ak Gid Etid la ke ou ka telechaje nan paj USCIS la.

Pou konprann relasyon ant kesyon yo ak Gid Etid la, tanpri itilize referans kwaze a ki bay ansanm ak materyèl sa yo, Resous Otoetid pou Nouvo Egzamen Sitwayènte a.
