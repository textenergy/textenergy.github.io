Sentence Writing Practice

How to use this page:

Click the ▶ Play sentence button to hear a sentence. Listen carefully, then type the complete sentence in the box below it. Click Check to see your result.

Words that are incorrect will appear in red. Correct words appear in green. You may listen and try again as many times as you need.

Important: Use correct capitalization and punctuation — they are required on the USCIS writing test. The check is exact: spelling, capital letters, and the period at the end all count.
