Vocabulary for the Naturalization Interview: Self-Test 2

Read each sentence carefully. Then select the best choice that has a similar meaning.
1. habitually --- Michael is habitually late for work at the bank.
2. verify --- Can you verify this information?
3. marital status --- What is your marital status?
4. swear --- I swear to tell the truth.
5. registered --- John registered for an English class at the community college.
6. spouse --- Did your spouse go with you outside the United States last summer?
7. current home address --- What is your current home address?
8. date of birth --- What is your date of birth?
9. advocated --- Mary advocated to change the working conditions at her job.
10. failed to --- Jim failed to send in his taxes on time.
11. federal --- Is that a federal government building?
12. exempt --- Donna was exempt from the final exam.
13. prior --- Gary has prior experience teaching history.
14. pending --- The decision on your loan application is pending.
15. Have you ever --- Have you ever visited New York?
16. member --- Scott is a member of the Parent Teacher Association.
17. resident --- I am a resident of Texas.
18. requested --- John requested information about the citizenship test.
19. disability --- Barbara's disability made walking difficult for her.
20. dependents --- Do you have any dependents?
