Civics Questions and Answers for the 65/20 Special Consideration

These are the questions from the regular test providing by USCIS for those applicants over 65 who qualify by having 20 years or more of legal permanent residence status. Ten questions will be asked and a passing score is six correct answers. Modifications of the answers are generally acceptable as long as the meaning is clear.

Extracted from Civics questions and answers for the 65/20 special consideration with annotations of the question number in the bank of 128 questions for the 2025 text.

Audio of the files in English and Spanish (unofficial translations) are available by clicking the Audio links.

[IMPORTANT: Don't study the wrong questions! If you applied after October 20, 2025, you MUST study this set of questions from the 2025 test, not the questions in the old 100-question test.]
[IMPORTANTE: ¡No estudies las preguntas equivocadas! Si presentaste tu solicitud después del 20 de octubre de 2025, DEBES estudiar este conjunto de preguntas del examen de 2025, no las preguntas del antiguo examen de 100 preguntas.]
