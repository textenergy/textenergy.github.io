Practice Saying Contact Information
Practica como decir tu informacion de contacto

Phone number and email address practice for the naturalization interview  ·  Practica de numero de telefono y correo electronico para la entrevista

Type your phone number and email address below, then press Read Aloud to hear how to say each one in English. Phone numbers are spoken in groups; email addresses are spoken character by character, with "dot", "at", and "dash" spoken for punctuation.

Escribe tu numero de telefono y correo electronico, luego presiona Read Aloud para escuchar como decirlos en ingles.

Phone Number
Numero de telefono

Country code defaults to +1 (United States). For US numbers, type 10 digits and they will be grouped automatically. For other countries, type all digits and they will be read one by one.
El codigo de pais es +1 (Estados Unidos) por defecto. Para numeros de EE.UU., escribe 10 digitos. Para otros paises, escribe todos los digitos.

Email Address
Correo electronico

Each letter and number is spoken individually. The symbols @, ., and - are spoken as "at", "dot", and "dash".
Cada letra y numero se dice individualmente. Los simbolos @ . - se dicen como "at", "dot" y "dash".
