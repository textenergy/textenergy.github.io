Practice Saying Your Address
Practica como decir tu direccion

Speaking practice for the naturalization interview  ·  Practica de pronunciacion para la entrevista de naturalizacion

For the street number and name, tap the keys to hear each character as you enter it, or type directly. Use the dropdowns after the number keys to insert a street type (Street, Road…) or directional (NW, S…) — each is spoken aloud when selected. Enter your apartment or suite number below the street keys if needed. Press Read Street Address to hear the complete street line spoken with pauses.

For city, state, and ZIP, fill in the second section and press Read City, State and ZIP.

Para el numero y nombre de la calle, toca las teclas o escribe directamente. Usa los menus para insertar tipo de calle o direccion cardinal. Ingresa tu apartamento o suite si es necesario, luego presiona Read Street Address. Para ciudad, estado y codigo postal, completa la segunda seccion.
