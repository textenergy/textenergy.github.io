# textenergy.github.io

*Explorations in bilingual English/Spanish learning materials and related UI*

I tutor Spanish speakers in English for general conversation, specific work-related vocabulary and US citizenship preparation. My resources  are intended to be functional HTML sets with supporting CSS and minimal JS to provide interactivity. Anyone will be welcome to download the HTML to use for developing their own materials or just use them online as is.

**US Citizenship 2025 test preparation materials** (entry point https://textenergy.github.io/USCIS2025/index.html[https://textenergy.github.io/USCIS2025/index.html](https://textenergy.github.io/USCIS2025/index.html)) include *audio files *for learnings to practice listening comprehension and *interactive components* to test their knowledge. I am providing a version of the USCIS 2025 Study Guide materials pulled from https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/brochures/USCIS-2025-Civics-Test-Study-Guide.pdf[https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/brochures/USCIS-2025-Civics-Test-Study-Guide.pdf](https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/brochures/USCIS-2025-Civics-Test-Study-Guide.pdf) which anyone can download directly from the USCIS web page (https://www.uscis.gov/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources/study-for-the-test?/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources/study-for-the-test?page=1[https://www.uscis.gov/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources/study-for-the-test?/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources/study-for-the-test?page=1](https://www.uscis.gov/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources/study-for-the-test?/citizenship/find-study-materials-and-resources/study-for-the-test?page=1)). USCIS made some changes to the Study Guide to reflect some differences requested by the current administration, but they are NOT publishing a list of changes to the Study Guide or informing the public when they edit and republish the Study Guide. I will be periodically checking the USCIS resources and will update my online files as needed.

**Daily life materials** will be developed around a few main themes: *Dairy work*, *landscaping*, and *general life skills/conversation*. I will make interactive Spanish/English resources for these topics over time.
